Game Instructions

Player 1 (Pink Monster)

  • Move Left: A
  • Move Right: D
  • Jump: W
  • Attack: Spacebar

Player 2 (Blue Monster)

  • Move Left: Left Arrow
  • Move Right: Right Arrow
  • Jump: Up Arrow
  • Attack: Down Arrow

Game Objective: Defeat the computer-controlled enemy by avoiding its attacks and landing successful attacks on it. Each successful attack reduces the enemy's health.

Player Movements: Use the respective keys or arrows to move left or right. Press the jump key to evade attacks or reach higher platforms.

Attacking: Press the attack key to strike the enemy. Time your attacks carefully to avoid getting hit by the enemy's punches.

Avoiding Enemy Attacks: Watch out for the enemy's punches aimed at your character. Use your movement and jumping skills to dodge incoming attacks.

Health and Victory: Your health is displayed at the top of the screen. Reduce the enemy's health to zero to win the game.

Game Over: If your health reaches zero before defeating the enemy, it's game over! Keep practicing to improve your skills and defeat the enemy.

Have fun and good luck!
